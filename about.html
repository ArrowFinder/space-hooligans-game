ABOUT SPACE HOOLIGANS

A Web3 arcade game where skill meets blockchain, and every shot counts in the cosmic battle for glory.

REWARDS

30% of every game transaction goes straight into a reward pool for players.

At the end of each season, that pool gets distributed to the Top 25 players on the leaderboard 1st place takes the lion's share, 25th gets a taste, and everyone in between gets their cut based on rank.

Play hard. Climb high. Get rewarded.

WHY?

I built this game using AI—start to finish. No team, no funding. Just curiosity, tools, and time. The point is simple: anyone can do this.

StudioChain is on the way to KARRAT, and when it launches, projects like this can live on a chain built for games. It's not running there yet, but one day you'll just swap the address and boom—better, faster, cheaper. That's the idea.

SPLIT

I'll probably use the BurnBolster contract when it goes live. But for now? Whatever ends up in the wallet at the end of the season will be split something like this:

30% to Season Rewards

20% to a Burn Address

50% to the Creator

That's the rough plan—subject to vibes and gas fees.

FUTURE

Things could evolve. Maybe this turns into more. Maybe it stays a side quest. Either way, I'll keep you posted.
